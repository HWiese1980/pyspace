List of all Nodes¶

pySPACE comes along with a big choice of 293 processing nodes. This includes numerous wrappers around optional external libraries. They can be accessed via the NodeChainOperation. In the following you can get an overview on their functionality, the mapping from node names in specification files to the node class and vice versa.

For details on the usage of the nodes and for getting usage examples, have a look at their documentation.
